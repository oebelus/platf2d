There is an infinite number of levels, and thus, the score will be infinite as well.

In each level, there will be a random number of coins, that you should find a way to gather. And if gathering them all is impossible, then, refresh the page and start again, with probably, a better luck in coins' positions?

After gathering all the coins of the level - good luck with this-, a door will be displayed, maybe, a door to a better random distribution of platforms? What if the door itself isn't accessible? Who knows...
And the game loop continues...
